# Napoleon’s Continental System as Proto-Central-Bank Warfare (1806–1814)

The Decree explicitly barred British goods and neutral vessels carrying them, aiming to choke gold remittances that averaged 15–20% of the Bank's annual £20 million gold reserves from European trade balances.
