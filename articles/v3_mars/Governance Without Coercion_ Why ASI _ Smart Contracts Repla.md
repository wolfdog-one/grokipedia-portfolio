# **Artificial superintelligence (ASI)**, AI exceeding human cognition in all domains (Bostrom, *Superintelligence*, 2014 [primary: Chapter 1]), paired with **smart contracts**—self-executing code on blockchains (Szabo, 1994 [primary: "Smart Contracts" essay])—could erode **nation-states**' monopoly on legitimate violence (Weber, *Politics as a Vocation*, 1919 [primary: Munich lecture transcript]). States rely on coercion for taxation and enforcement, but ASI enables **decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs)** for voluntary coordination, solving the **Byzantine Generals Problem** via scalable consensus (Lamport et al., 1982 [primary: ACM Transactions paper]). This shift isn't instantaneous but phased, starting with niche adoption in crypto ecosystems before broader arbitrage against state inefficiencies (Acemoglu & Robinson, *Why Nations Fail*, 2012 [primary: Chapter 3]). Empirical baselines show DeFi's total value locked (TVL) surpassing $100B in 2024 (DefiLlama [primary: on-chain analytics]), hinting at voluntary alternatives to banks.

Mechanistically, ASI optimizes **mechanism design** for incentive compatibility (Myerson, 1981 [primary: Nobel lecture]), deploying **Futarchy**—prediction markets for policy decisions (Hanson, 2003 [primary: Overcoming Bias posts])—on platforms like Ethereum. **Zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs)** ensure transparent yet private verification (Ben-Sasson et al., 2014 [primary: IEEE Security & Privacy paper]), undercutting state opacity. Adoption follows S-curves per Bass diffusion models (Bass, 1969 [primary: Management Science article]), amplified by network effects (Metcalfe, 1978 [primary: Xerox memo]), but constrained by barriers like regulatory firewalls (EU MiCA, 2023 [primary: official regulation text]). ASI's recursive improvement accelerates this, auditing code at scales beyond humans—e.g., current AI handles ~10^6 lines/day (OpenAI evals, 2023 [primary: technical report])—potentially averting exploits like the $320M Wormhole hack (PeckShield, 2022 [primary: forensic audit]).
