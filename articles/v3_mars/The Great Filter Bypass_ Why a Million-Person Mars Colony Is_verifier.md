## Great Filter Stages

**Filters** choke cosmic expansion at key points [VERIFIED] Exact source: [2] Hanson's model delineates Filters as low-probability events impeding the transition from rare life to galaxy-spanning civilizations. Abiogenesis odds hover at 10^{-20} to 10^{-40} per habitable zone, per Bayesian priors [VERIFIED] Exact source: [4] Kipping's PNAS (2020) uses Bayesian inference on the Drake equation to estimate abiogenesis probability <10^{-10} in optimistic cases, but broader priors (incorporating [5]) yield ranges down to 10^{-40} for rare chemical origins in habitable zones; [5] Spiegel & Turner (PNAS, 2012) similarly constrains early life emergence via exoplanet survey Bayes factors. Multicellularity took Earth 3 billion years, likely a universal rarity due to energetic bottlenecks [VERIFIED] Exact source: [6] Szathmáry & Maynard Smith (1995) highlight major transitions like multicellularity as improbable due to compartmentalization challenges; [7] Knoll (2011) details Earth's timeline (~3 Gya for prokaryotes to ~0.6 Gya for complex multicellularity) and energetic constraints (e.g., oxygen levels, symbiosis). Intelligence demands stable oxygen >15% and climates, while expansion risks self-destruction [PARTIALLY VERIFIED] Exact source: [2] discusses intelligence and expansion risks (e.g., self-destruction via tech); oxygen >15% is supported by geobiological models (e.g., Berner’s COPSE in [7]), but no single citation here mandates exactly >15%—it's a threshold from paleoatmospheric reconstructions for large-brain metabolism.

- **Pre-Biotic Filter:** Rare self-replicating molecules (N=1 on Earth) [VERIFIED] Exact source: [2] and [4] frame abiogenesis as a rare pre-biotic event, with Earth's single instance (N=1) as evidence of low probability.
- **Eukaryote Filter:** Mitochondria fusion, odds <10^{-10} [UNVERIFIED] Exact source: [6] describes endosymbiosis (mitochondria) as a key transition but does not quantify odds at <10^{-10}; probabilistic estimates derive from transition rarity in [2] and Bayesian astrobiology ([4]), but the specific figure appears unsubstantiated here—more qualitative rarity.
- **Intelligence Filter:** Tool-use in social primates, post-dinosaur era [VERIFIED] Exact source: [2] posits intelligence as a late-stage Filter after ~4 Gya of life, aligning with Earth's post-Cretaceous tool-using hominids (~2-3 Mya).
- **Expansion Filter:** Tech traps like nuclear war or rogue AI [VERIFIED] Exact source: [3] details AI misalignment as a post-intelligence trap; [2] includes self-destruction in expansion phase.

Probabilistic models suggest we're past early filters but pre-expansion, with 80% risks planet-tied [UNVERIFIED] Exact source: [8] Sandberg et al. (arXiv, 2018) uses Monte Carlo simulations on Drake parameters to dissolve the Paradox, suggesting we're likely early but does not specify "80% planet-tied risks"—it models Filter placements with ~50-90% probability post-intelligence for some scenarios; the 80% figure may be an approximation but lacks exact match.
