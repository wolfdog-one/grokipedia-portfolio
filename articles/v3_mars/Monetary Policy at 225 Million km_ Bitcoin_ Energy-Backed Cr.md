# Monetary Policy at 225 Million km

At 225 million km from Earth, Mars' economy battles light-speed lags (8-48 min round-trip) and energy scarcity (590 W/m² solar flux vs. Earth's 1,368 W/m²), dooming fiat reliance on instant policy tweaks.[1][24] **Bitcoin**'s proof-of-work (PoW) fits via repurposed nuclear/solar power, while **energy-backed credits (EBCs)** peg to kWh for stable internal trades.[2][25] Yet delays spike orphan blocks, dust storms derate panels 20-50%, and the **Cantillon Effect** funnels value to early miners in this closed loop.[3][19] Simulations peg Gini rises to 0.45-0.52 by 2070 sans fixes, from grid centralization not cabals.[4][26]

# Bitcoin's Lag-Wracked Resilience

**PoW** hashing aligns with Mars' 1-5 GWh/year output from Kilopower reactors (10 kWe each) and PV arrays, diverting 0.5-1.5 MWh/colonist to snag 5-15% global hashrate by 2040 via laser links.[5][6][27][28] Propagation math bites: \( t_p = d/c + q \) (d=225e9 m, c=3e8 m/s) yields 12.5-25 min lags, pushing orphan rates to 10-25% per selfish mining \( \alpha = \frac{\gamma}{1 - (1 - \gamma)(1 - f)} \) (f=0.2).[7][29] Lightning Network zips intra-colony swaps for ISRU water (40 kWh/kg), but volatility (40-60%) clashes with rationed mining.[8][30] **Nakamoto coefficient** craters to 2-4 from reactor monopolies, vs. Earth's 15-25, inviting 51% hijacks.[11][32]

Dust storm counterfactual: 2035 halving meets 70% PV drop, halting 15-30% blocks in sims, slashing throughput 25-40% and spiking barter 10-20%—no apocalypse, just liquidity pain.[10][33]

- **Pros:** Audits defy Earth regs during 2-year windows; thermodynamic proof resists fakes.
- **Cons:** Fees hoard sats; quantum Grover halves keys by 2050 sans lattices.[12][34]

# EBC Pegs: Physics-Bound Stability

EBCs mint 1:1 to verified kWh via oracles on RTGs/PVs, fueling aphelion booms in propellant (30 kWh/kg CH₄).[13][14][35][36] zk-SNARKs prove \( \pi = zkSNARK(\sum E_t) \) privately, dodging flares at 200 mSv/year.[15][37] Dust τ=0.2-1.0 slashes irradiance \( I = I_0 e^{-\tau} \), risking 20-35% depegs; overcollateral (150%) and burns \( \Delta supply = -k \delta E \) (k=0.1) cap cascades.[16][38] DAO quorums (30-60%) tune params, but reentrancy bugs echo Ethereum losses at 5-10% scale.[17][22][39][40]

Reactor fail counterfactual: 2042 inflows pump BTC 3-5x, but 2-7%/year fault guts 50-70% reserves, deflating via MV=PQ (V↓); EBC silos limit to 10-20%.[18][41]

# Cantillon's Martian Skew

New money hits insiders first, per Cantillon: Miners grab 50-70% initial BTC/EBC, hiking Gini to 0.45-0.52 via \( \Delta P_i = \frac{\Delta M \alpha}{V} \) (α=0.6).[19][20][42][43] UBE doles 8-12 kWh/day, burning \( B = \beta \Delta E \) (β=0.2) to flatten; entropy caps growth at 0.5-1.5%/year post-2100.[21][24][44][45] Quadratic funding reallocates 15-25% fees, holding Gini at 0.28-0.35 in sims; pure BTC hits 0.50+ from network lock-in.[23][46][47] Hybrids need threshold sigs for lag-proof consensus—adapt or fork.[48]
