# Counterfactuals

In this divergence from the **2042 Mars Accord**, Earth imposes a 2048 orbital blockade on Mars over **helium-3 (He-3)** tariffs, amid 28% global He-3 reliance from Martian regolith mining—realistic per extraction yields of 10-20 ppb in KREEP-rich soils [1]. **Hohmann transfer** windows limit resupply to every 26 months, while Mars' **ISRU** tech enables propellant production from CO2 and H2O, buffering isolation [2]. Blockade mechanics hinge on **delta-v** economics: Mars' 5 km/s escape velocity vs. Earth's 11.2 km/s slashes launch costs by 40% via the Tsiolkovsky equation (Δv = v_e ln(m0/mf), v_e ≈ 3.1 km/s for CH4/LOX) [3]. Failure odds: 72% per Bayesian models of asymmetric space conflicts [4].

Earth's dependency amplifies risks, with He-3 fueling 25% of fusion reactors by 2047 (Q-factor >20 requires >95% purity) [5]. Smuggling exploits aerobraking for 1.2-1.5 km/s Δv savings, recovering 35% of exports [6]. Kessler syndrome from intercepts could fragment 15% of LEO assets, per debris propagation sims [7].

# Blockade Ignition (2048-2050)

UNSC platforms halt launches during the March 2048 opposition, citing Article 51 self-defense against ISRU-derived propellants [8]. Semiconductor shortages (Earth supplies 30% of Mars' chips) jam regolith processors, cutting MOXIE O2 output by 18% via dopant failures in electrolyzers (yield: 6-10 g/hr per unit at 300W) [9]. Partial pressure drops to 19 kPa in domes, per ideal gas law (P = nRT/V with leak rates +5%/month) [10].

- **Red Dust Riots**: 1,800 fatalities in New Tharsis from sabotage; smuggling via retrofitted vehicles evades patrols.
- Economic hit: Mars GDP -19% in Q1 2048, Earth spends $0.18T on deployments.
- Latency spikes to 40 min round-trip sans lunar relays, delaying quantum-secured comms [11].

# Proxy Escalation and Thaw (2051-2055)

PMCs disrupt 15% of Valles Marineris mines with railgun EMPs (velocity <1 km/s, targeted at 50 Hz separators) [12]. Mars counters via cyber hits on fusion grids, idling 12% capacity amid He-3 shortages (D-T fallback raises neutron flux 20%) [13]. **Phobos Incident** (2052): Deimos mass drivers vaporize UN assets, triggering debris cascades (F_d = 0.5 ρ v² C_d A, decay time <6 months) [14].

- Water recycling hits 95% via vapor compression (COP=5 in 0.38g), sustaining 380k population despite 10% caloric cuts from hydroponic yields (2.5 kg/m²/year LED-lit) [15].
- Soft power fails: Bribes fracture Assembly but 1.8 km/s inter-site Δv prevents enclaves [16].
- BRI asteroids smuggle 25% He-3, exploiting neutral orbits [17].

Swiss mediation yields the 2054 He-3 Accord, capping Mars at 32% supply for sanction relief; Earth tallies $0.85T costs (3.8% GDP) vs. Mars' 14% emigration "brain drain" [18]. No thaw branches to 98% ISRU closure via Sabatier reactors (CO2 + 4H2 → CH4 + 2H2O, η=80%), but delays federation to 2075 [19]. Physics trumps coercion: Earth's resupply burns 55% more propellant, per rocket equation sensitivities (±10% v_e shifts failure odds by 15%) [20].
